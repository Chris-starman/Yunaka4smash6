Review on ARMS

Nintendo's other Fighting Game has a spring in its step

Anyone remember how Punch-Out on the Wii? Well, imagine making a stretching that into a game with projectiles and there you have ARMS. It's an Arena Fighter that'll have you competing in matches to bob and weave around long, stretchy, colorful punches, or thowing your own to avoid wide, stretchy grabs. Whatever I did, it always kept me coming back for more.
